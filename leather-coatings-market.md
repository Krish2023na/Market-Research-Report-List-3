<p><strong>The Leather Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solventborne</li><li>Waterborne</li><li>UV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Leather coatings are available in three main types: solventborne, waterborne, and UV coatings. Solventborne coatings contain solvents that help the coating to be applied smoothly and evenly. Waterborne coatings use water as a solvent, making them more environmentally friendly and easy to clean up. UV coatings are cured using ultraviolet light, offering fast drying times and excellent durability. Each type of coating has its own advantages and suitability for different applications in the leather industry.</p></p>
