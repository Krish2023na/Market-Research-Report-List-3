<p><p>Pantograph carbon strips are widely used in electric trains for collecting power from overhead lines. There are two main types of pantograph carbon strips: metal-impregnated carbon strips and pure graphite carbon strips. Metal-impregnated carbon strips are a combination of carbon and metal powders, providing enhanced conductivity and wear resistance. Pure graphite carbon strips, on the other hand, are made solely from graphite, offering high conductivity and minimal wear. Both types of carbon strips are crucial components in ensuring efficient power collection in electric trains.</p></p>
