<p><p>The residential solar battery market can be divided into two main types: Independent Type and Integrated Type. Independent Type batteries are standalone systems that are not connected to a household's existing solar panel system. They are typically used as backup power sources during outages. Integrated Type batteries, on the other hand, are connected to a household's existing solar panel system and are designed to store excess energy generated during the day for use at night or during periods of low sunlight.</p></p>
