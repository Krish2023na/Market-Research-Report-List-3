<p><p>Light beams market types include parallel beam and concentric beam. Parallel beam is a type of market where multiple beams of light run parallel to each other, creating a uniform and consistent lighting effect. Concentric beam market, on the other hand, involves beams of light that converge towards a central point, creating a focused and intense lighting effect. Both types of markets offer unique lighting solutions for different applications, catering to a wide range of lighting needs in various industries.</p></p>
