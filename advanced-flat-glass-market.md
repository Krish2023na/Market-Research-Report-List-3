<p><p>Advanced flat glass market types include toughened glass, wired glass, and laminated glass. Toughened glass is heat-treated to increase its strength, making it ideal for use in safety-critical applications. Wired glass has embedded metal wires for added strength and fire resistance. Laminated glass consists of two or more layers of glass with an interlayer to enhance durability and security. These advanced flat glass types cater to a wide range of industries including construction, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
