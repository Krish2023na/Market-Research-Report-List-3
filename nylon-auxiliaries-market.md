<p><p>The Nylon Auxiliaries market offers a range of types including Cross Linking Agents, Emulsifiers, Fixing Agents, Dispersing Agents, Defoamers, and others. Cross Linking Agents are used to improve the strength and durability of nylon materials. Emulsifiers help in the uniform dispersion of ingredients in nylon production. Fixing Agents help in setting the dye in the nylon fabric. Dispersing Agents aid in the even distribution of dyes and pigments. Defoamers prevent unwanted foam formation during the production process. Other types of auxiliaries play various roles in enhancing the quality and properties of nylon products.</p></p>
