<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The UV Inspection Lights Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UVA Lights</li><li>UVB Lights</li><li>UVC Lights</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UV Inspection Lights market is segmented into three types based on the wavelength of ultraviolet rays emitted: UVA Lights, UVB Lights, and UVC Lights. UVA Lights have a longer wavelength and are commonly used for fluorescent penetrant inspection and magnetic particle inspection. UVB Lights have a medium wavelength and are often used for fluorescence inspection in the medical and industrial fields. UVC Lights have the shortest wavelength and are primarily used for germicidal applications, such as disinfection and sterilization. Each type of UV Inspection Light serves different purposes in various industries.</p></p>
