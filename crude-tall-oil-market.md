<p><ul><li>Softwood Crude Tall Oil</li><li>Mixed Crude Tall Oil</li><li>Hardwood Crude Tall Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crude Tall Oil is a co-product of the kraft pulping process used to make paper. The three main types are Softwood Crude Tall Oil, Mixed Crude Tall Oil, and Hardwood Crude Tall Oil. Softwood Crude Tall Oil is derived from softwood pulp and is typically high in rosin content. Mixed Crude Tall Oil is a combination of softwood and hardwood pulping byproducts. Hardwood Crude Tall Oil comes from hardwood pulp and has different chemical properties compared to softwood varieties. Each type has its own market demand and applications in various industries.</p></p>
