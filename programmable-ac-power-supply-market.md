<p><p>Programmable AC power supplies come in three main market types: Single-Output Type, Dual-Output Type, and Multiple-Output Type. A Single-Output Type power supply provides one output channel for testing purposes, while a Dual-Output Type has two independent output channels for more flexibility in testing. Multiple-Output Type power supplies offer three or more channels, allowing for simultaneous testing of multiple devices. Each type caters to different testing requirements, offering varying levels of output channels to meet the needs of different applications and industries.</p></p>
