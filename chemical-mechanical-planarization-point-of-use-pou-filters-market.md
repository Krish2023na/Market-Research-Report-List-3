<p><p>Chemical Mechanical Planarization Point-of-Use (POU) Filters are categorized based on their removal rating, which determines the size of particles they can filter out. Filters with a removal rating less than 0.5 µm are used for ultrafine particle filtration, while filters with a rating between 0.5 µm and 1 µm are suitable for filtering slightly larger particles. Those with a removal rating between 1 µm and 5 µm are designed for filtering medium-sized particles, and filters with a rating over 5 µm are used for larger particle removal.</p></p>
