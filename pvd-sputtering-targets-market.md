<p><p>PVD sputtering targets market is segmented into metal sputtering targets and compound sputtering targets. Metal sputtering targets consist of pure metals like gold, silver, and copper, while compound sputtering targets are made of complex materials such as oxides, nitrides, and carbides. Metal sputtering targets are commonly used for depositing metal films, while compound sputtering targets are preferred for creating thin films with specific properties like corrosion resistance, hardness, and conductivity. Both types cater to different requirements in various industries like electronics, optics, and automotive.</p></p>
