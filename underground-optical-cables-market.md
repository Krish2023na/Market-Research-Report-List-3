<p><p>Underground optical cables market types refer to the two main categories of fiber optic cables used for underground installations: multimode fiber and singlemode fiber. Multimode fiber cables are designed for shorter distances and are typically used in LAN, data center, and campus applications. Singlemode fiber cables are used for long-distance transmissions and are commonly deployed in metropolitan networks, telecommunication systems, and internet backbones. Both types of cables are essential for providing reliable and high-speed communication in underground settings.</p></p>
