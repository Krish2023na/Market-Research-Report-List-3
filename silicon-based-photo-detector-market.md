<p><p>Silicon Drift Detectors (SDDs) and Silicon PhotoMultipliers (SiPMs) are two types of silicon-based photodetectors that are commonly used in various applications. SDDs offer high energy resolution and fast response times, making them ideal for use in X-ray spectrometry and electron microscopy. SiPMs, on the other hand, offer high photon detection efficiency and single photon sensitivity, making them suitable for applications such as medical imaging and LiDAR systems. Both types of detectors play a crucial role in enabling advanced sensing and detection technologies.</p></p>
