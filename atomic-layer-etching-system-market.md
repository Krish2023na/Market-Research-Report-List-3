<p><p>Atomic Layer Etching (ALE) Systems are used for precise etching of materials in advanced semiconductor manufacturing. There are two main types of ALE Systems: Plasma Type and High Temperature Type. </p><p>Plasma Type systems use plasma chemistry to etch materials layer by layer, ensuring high precision and control. On the other hand, High Temperature Type systems use elevated temperatures to facilitate the etching process without the need for plasma. Both types of systems offer unique advantages and are preferred in different applications based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
