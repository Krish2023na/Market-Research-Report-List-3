<p><p>Home oxygen therapy devices market consists of two main types: oxygen source equipment and oxygen delivery devices. Oxygen source equipment includes oxygen concentrators and liquid oxygen systems, which generate and store oxygen for use by patients. Oxygen delivery devices include nasal cannulas, oxygen masks, and oxygen cylinders, which deliver the oxygen from the source equipment to the patient. These devices are essential for patients with respiratory conditions who require supplemental oxygen to maintain adequate oxygen levels in their blood.</p></p>
