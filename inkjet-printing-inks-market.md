<p><p>Inkjet Printing Inks market consists of two main types: Drop On Demand (DOD) Inkjet Inks and Continuous Inkjet Inks. DOD inks are used in inkjet printers that only dispense ink droplets when needed, resulting in more precise printing. Continuous inks are used in printers that continuously jet out ink onto the printing surface. Both types of inks have their own specific applications and are used in various industries such as packaging, textiles, and advertising.</p></p>
