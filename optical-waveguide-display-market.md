<p><ul><li>Channel Waveguide</li><li>Planar Waveguide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical waveguide display market includes two main types: channel waveguide and planar waveguide. Channel waveguides guide light through a specific path created by a channel with higher refractive index material. Planar waveguides guide light on a flat surface with alternating layers of materials of different refractive indices. Both types are used in the development of advanced display technologies, such as augmented reality glasses and virtual reality headsets. These technologies offer users a more immersive and high-quality visual experience compared to traditional displays.</p></p>
