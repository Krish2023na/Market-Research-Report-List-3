<p><strong>The Flame Retardant Polyester Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inherent FRP Staple Fiber</li><li>Treated FRP Staple Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inherent flame retardant polyester (FRP) staple fiber refers to fibers that are inherently resistant to flames without the need for additional treatment. On the other hand, treated FRP staple fiber involves fibers that have been treated with flame retardant chemicals to improve their fire resistance properties. Both types of fibers are utilized in various industries such as textiles, automotive, and construction for applications requiring flame retardant qualities. The choice between the two types depends on specific requirements and end-use applications.</p></p>
