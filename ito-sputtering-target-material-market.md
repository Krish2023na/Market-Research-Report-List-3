<p><strong>The ITO Sputtering Target Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plane Target</li><li>Rotating Target</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ITO sputtering target materials are used in thin film deposition processes for various applications. Plane targets are stationary and provide a large surface area for uniform coating, making them ideal for high-volume production. Rotating targets, on the other hand, spin during deposition to ensure even erosion and prolong target life. They are commonly used in high-energy applications where high deposition rates are required. Both types of target materials play a vital role in ensuring efficient and precise thin film deposition processes.</p></p>
