<p><p>Electrical house (E-House) units are pre-assembled, pre-tested modular power equipment enclosures designed for quick deployment in diverse applications. Low voltage E-Houses cater to applications requiring voltage levels below 1 kV, commonly used in residential and commercial settings. Medium voltage E-Houses are designed for applications requiring voltage levels between 1-38 kV, commonly found in industrial and utility settings. Both types offer fast installation, reduced on-site labor and downtime, and improved safety measures, making them cost-effective solutions for power distribution needs.</p></p>
