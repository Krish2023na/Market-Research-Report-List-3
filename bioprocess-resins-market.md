The market is segmented into three main types: Protein A Chromatography Resins, Anion Exchange Resins, and Cation Exchange Resins. Protein A Chromatography Resins are primarily used for the purification of monoclonal antibodies. Anion Exchange Resins selectively bind negatively charged molecules, while Cation Exchange Resins bind positively charged molecules. These resins play a crucial role in the downstream processing of biopharmaceuticals, ensuring high purity and yield.</p></p>
