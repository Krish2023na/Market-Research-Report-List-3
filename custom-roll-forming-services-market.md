<p><strong>The Custom Roll Forming Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Roll Forming</li><li>Hot Roll Forming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Custom roll forming services cater to different market types, namely cold roll forming and hot roll forming. Cold roll forming involves shaping metal sheets at room temperature, resulting in high precision and tight tolerances. It is ideal for producing complex shapes with excellent surface finish. On the other hand, hot roll forming involves heating metal sheets above their recrystallization temperature, allowing for easier shaping and forming of thicker materials. Both market types offer unique advantages and cater to specific requirements of different industries.</p></p>
