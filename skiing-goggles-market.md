<p><p>Skiing goggles come in various types based on the shape of the lens: Cylindrical Lens, Spherical Lens, and Toric Lens. Cylindrical lenses are flat and offer a wide field of vision but may cause distortion. Spherical lenses curve horizontally and vertically for a more natural view without distortion. Toric lenses have a curved shape that mimics the curvature of the eye for enhanced peripheral vision. Each type caters to different preferences and needs of skiers.</p></p>
