<p><p>A self-ligating bracket is a type of orthodontic bracket that does not require elastic or metal ties to hold the archwire in place. Instead, it utilizes a small sliding door mechanism to hold the wire, allowing for easier adjustment and reduced friction. This results in faster treatment times, less discomfort, and improved oral hygiene compared to traditional braces.</p><p>The self-ligating bracket market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years, with a projected CAGR of 14.1% during the forecast period. The increasing prevalence of malocclusions and the growing demand for aesthetically pleasing orthodontic treatments are driving the adoption of self-ligating brackets. Additionally, advancements in technology and the development of modern materials have led to more efficient and comfortable bracket systems, further fueling market growth.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the self-ligating bracket market include the introduction of customized bracket systems, the integration of digital technologies for treatment planning, and the rising popularity of clear or ceramic bracket options.
