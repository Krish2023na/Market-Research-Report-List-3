Oil coolers help regulate the temperature of the engine oil, preventing overheating and maintaining lubrication efficiency. Intercoolers are used in turbocharged engines to cool the compressed air before it enters the engine, improving performance and efficiency. Each type of heat exchanger serves a specific purpose in ensuring the smooth operation of an automobile engine.</p></p>
