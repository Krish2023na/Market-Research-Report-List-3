<p><ul><li>Class-A</li><li>Class-B</li><li>Class-A/B</li><li>Class-D</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Light vehicle audio amplifiers come in different types such as Class-A, Class-B, Class-A/B, and Class-D. Class-A amplifiers are known for their high-quality sound but are less efficient. Class-B amplifiers are more efficient but can cause distortion at low volumes. Class-A/B amplifiers combine the best of both Class-A and Class-B, offering good sound quality and efficiency. Class-D amplifiers are the most efficient but can have lower sound quality compared to other classes. Each type caters to different preferences and needs in the light vehicle audio amplifier market.</p></p>
