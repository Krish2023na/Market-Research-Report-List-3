<p><p>Rainwater filters are essential for maintaining the quality of collected rainwater. Mechanical pre-tank filters are used to remove large debris before water enters the tank. Microscopic filters remove smaller particles from the water. Disinfection filters eliminate harmful bacteria and viruses. Carbon filters remove impurities and improve taste. Other types of filters may include UV filters, reverse osmosis filters, and ion exchange filters. Each type serves a specific purpose in ensuring that rainwater is clean and safe for various uses.</p></p>
