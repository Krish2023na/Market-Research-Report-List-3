<p><strong>The Acoustic Wave Biosensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Quartz Crystal Microbalance (QCM) Sensor</li><li>Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acoustic wave biosensors are used for detecting biomolecular interactions in various applications. Quartz Crystal Microbalance (QCM) sensors utilize the piezoelectric properties of quartz crystals to measure small changes in mass, providing highly sensitive detection. Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) sensors use acoustic waves traveling along the surface of a piezoelectric substrate to detect binding events. Both types of sensors offer label-free, real-time detection capabilities, making them valuable tools in the fields of medical diagnostics, environmental monitoring, and biotechnology.</p></p>
