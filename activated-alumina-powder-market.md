<p><p>Activated alumina powder is categorized based on particle size into three main types: 80-150 mesh, 150-300 mesh, and above 300 mesh. The 80-150 mesh activated alumina powder is commonly used for gas and liquid dehydration due to its larger particle size. The 150-300 mesh powder is suitable for applications requiring higher surface area and pore volume. The above 300 mesh powder is ideal for fine polishing and catalyst support due to its smaller particle size and higher purity. Each type caters to specific industrial requirements.</p></p>
