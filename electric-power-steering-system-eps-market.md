<p><p>Electric Power Steering System (EPS) market can be divided into several types based on the technology used: Column-EPS (C-EPS), Pinion-EPS (P-EPS), Rack-EPS (R-EPS), and Dual Pinion-EPS (DP-EPS). C-EPS uses a single electric motor mounted on the steering column, P-EPS uses an electric motor directly attached to the steering rack, R-EPS integrates the motor into the steering rack, and DP-EPS uses two motors to provide steering assistance. Each type offers different benefits in terms of cost, packaging, and performance.</p></p>
