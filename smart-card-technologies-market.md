<p><p>Smart Card Technologies market comprises two main types: Contact Card and Contactless Card. Contactless Card, on the other hand, uses radio-frequency identification (RFID) technology for data transmission without the need for direct contact. This type of card is commonly used in public transportation systems and payment cards for quicker and more convenient transactions.</p></p>
