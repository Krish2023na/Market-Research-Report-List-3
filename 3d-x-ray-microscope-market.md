<p><strong>The 3D X-ray Microscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transmission X-Ray Microscope</li><li>Scanning X-Ray Microscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>3D X-ray Microscope Market includes two main types: Transmission X-ray Microscope and Scanning X-ray Microscope. Transmission X-ray Microscope is used to create 2D and 3D images by passing X-rays through a sample. It is ideal for examining internal structures of materials. On the other hand, Scanning X-ray Microscope uses a focused X-ray beam to generate high-resolution images of the surface of a sample. This type is useful for studying surface features and analyzing the composition of materials. Both types play a crucial role in scientific research and industrial applications.</p></p>
