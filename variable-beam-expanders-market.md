<p><p>Variable beam expanders are optical devices used to increase the diameter of a laser beam. They are available in two main market types: those made from fused quartz material and those made from optical glass material. Fused quartz material beam expanders offer high temperature resistance, low thermal expansion, and excellent optical properties. On the other hand, optical glass material beam expanders provide good transmission, low cost, and ease of manufacturing. Both types cater to different industrial needs depending on their specific requirements and budget constraints.</p></p>
