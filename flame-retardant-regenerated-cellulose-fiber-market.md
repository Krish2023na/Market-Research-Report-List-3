<p><p>Flame-retardant regenerated cellulose fiber market types include phosphorus type, silicon nitrogen type, and others. Phosphorus type flame retardants contain phosphorus compounds that inhibit combustion by releasing water vapor and forming a protective char layer. Silicon nitrogen type flame retardants work by releasing inert gases to dilute oxygen and hinder combustion. Other types of flame retardants may utilize different mechanisms to prevent or slow down the spread of fire in regenerated cellulose fibers. Each type offers specific advantages and applications in flame-retardant textiles.</p></p>
