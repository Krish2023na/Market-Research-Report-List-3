<p><strong>The Flame Retardant Polypropylene Granule Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Halogen Flame</li><li>Halogen Free Flame</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flame Retardant Polypropylene Granule market can be categorized into two main types: Halogen Flame and Halogen Free Flame. Halogen Flame products contain halogenated compounds which release hydrogen halides when burned, creating a flame-retardant effect. On the other hand, Halogen Free Flame products do not contain any halogenated compounds and rely on alternative methods such as phosphorus or nitrogen-based additives to achieve flame retardancy. Both types cater to different market needs and regulations, with Halogen Free Flame products gaining popularity due to environmental concerns.</p></p>
