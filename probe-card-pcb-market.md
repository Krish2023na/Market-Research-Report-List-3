<p><p>The Probe Card PCB market includes three main types: PTH Type, Buildup IVH Type, and Hybrid Type. PTH Type PCBs have plated through holes for better signal transmission. Buildup IVH Type PCBs use multiple layers of insulation for increased density and performance. Hybrid Type PCBs combine the features of both PTH and Buildup IVH types for optimal performance in complex applications. Each type offers specific advantages in terms of signal transmission, density, and overall performance in the Probe Card industry.</p></p>
