<p><strong>The Automotive Electric Scroll Compressors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hermetic Scroll Compressors</li><li>Semi-hermetic Scroll Compressors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hermetic Scroll Compressors are sealed systems where the motor and compressor are housed in a single unit, preventing any leakage of refrigerant. These compressors are commonly used in small to medium sized air conditioning and refrigeration systems. On the other hand, Semi-hermetic Scroll Compressors have a removable top cover which allows for easier maintenance and repair. They are often used in larger industrial applications where the compressor may need to be serviced or replaced. Both types play a vital role in the automotive electric scroll compressors market.</p></p>
