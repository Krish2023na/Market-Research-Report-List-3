<p><p>Antioxidants market types include Amines, Hindered Phenols, Phosphites, Thioesters, and others. Amines are organic compounds that prevent oxidation by scavenging free radicals. Hindered Phenols inhibit oxidation by donating hydrogen atoms to free radicals. Phosphites act as reducing agents, disrupting the oxidation process. Thioesters react with free radicals to prevent oxidation. Other types of antioxidants may include enzymes, vitamins, and minerals that also help protect against oxidative damage. Together, these antioxidants play a crucial role in preserving the quality and shelf life of various products.</p></p>
