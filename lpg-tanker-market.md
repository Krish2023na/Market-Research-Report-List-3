<p><ul><li>VLGC</li><li>LGS</li><li>MGC</li><li>SGC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>VLGC, or Very Large Gas Carriers, are the largest gas carriers and typically transport liquefied petroleum gas (LPG) in large quantities across long distances. LGS, or Large Gas Carriers, are smaller than VLGCs but still have a significant capacity for transporting LPG. MGC, or Medium Gas Carriers, are smaller vessels used for shorter or regional routes. SGC, or Small Gas Carriers, are the smallest gas carriers and are typically used for coastal or inland transportation of LPG. Each type of tanker serves a specific market segment based on size and capacity requirements.</p></p>
