<p><p>Molecular spectroscopy equipment market includes different types based on the techniques used for analysis. Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (NMR) spectrometers are used to study the molecular structure of compounds. UV-Visible spectrometers measure the absorption and transmission of light by molecules. Infrared (IR) spectrometers analyze the vibrations of molecules. Near Infrared (NIR) spectrometers are used for quantitative analysis of various compounds. Each type of equipment has specific applications and plays a crucial role in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, food, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
