<p><p>Thermoplastic Polyurethane Films (TPU) are widely used in various industries due to their flexibility, durability, and biocompatibility. There are different types of TPU films available in the market, including Polyester TPU, Polyether TPU, and Polycaprolactone TPU. Polyester TPU offers excellent resistance to chemicals and abrasion, while Polyether TPU provides superior flexibility and low-temperature performance. Polycaprolactone TPU is known for its biodegradability and excellent heat resistance. Each type of TPU film caters to different application needs in industries such as automotive, textiles, and healthcare.</p></p>
