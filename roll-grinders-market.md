<p><ul><li>Wheel-Traverse Type</li><li>Table-Traverse Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Roll grinders are used in various industries for precision grinding of rolls. There are two main types of roll grinders: Wheel-Traverse Type and Table-Traverse Type. Wheel-Traverse Type roll grinders use a grinding wheel that moves across the roll surface, while Table-Traverse Type roll grinders use a stationary grinding wheel and a moving table. Each type has its own advantages and applications depending on the size and shape of the rolls being ground. Both types are essential for achieving high accuracy and surface finish in roll grinding processes.</p></p>
