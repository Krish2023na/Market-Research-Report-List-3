<p><p>Digital Network Analyzers are categorized based on frequency range: Below 1.5GHz, 1.5-4GHz, 4-10GHz, and Above 10GHz. Below 1.5GHz analyzers are suitable for low-frequency applications like power measurements and distortion analysis. The 1.5-4GHz range is commonly used in wireless communication systems. Analyzers in the 4-10GHz range are ideal for radar systems and satellite communications. Above 10GHz analyzers are specialized for high-frequency applications such as microwave and millimeter-wave technologies. Each market segment caters to specific industries and applications requiring different frequency ranges.</p></p>
