<p><p>Wood based panels market encompasses various types of products including particleboard (PB), medium density fibreboard (MDF), high density fibreboard (HDF), oriented strand board (OSB), and plywood. Particleboard is made from wood particles bonded together, MDF is made from fine wood fibers pressed together, HDF is a denser version of MDF, OSB is made from strands of wood aligned in layers, and plywood is made from thin layers of wood veneer glued together. Each type of wood based panel offers unique characteristics and applications in the construction and furniture industries.</p></p>
