<p><p>Concealed slides are commonly found in two main market types: full extension and single extension. In full extension slides, the drawer fully extends out of the cabinet, allowing for easy access to the contents inside. This type is often used in kitchen cabinets or storage units where full visibility and access are desired. On the other hand, single extension slides only partially extend the drawer out of the cabinet. This type is typically used in office furniture or smaller cabinets where space may be limited.</p></p>
