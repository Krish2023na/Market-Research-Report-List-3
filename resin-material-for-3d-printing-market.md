<p><p>Resin material for 3D printing is available in two main types in the market: liquid resin and solid resin. Liquid resin is commonly used in SLA (stereolithography) printers and is in a liquid state before curing under UV light. Solid resin, on the other hand, is typically used in DLP (digital light processing) printers and is already in solid form before printing. Both types of resin offer different advantages and characteristics, catering to various needs and applications in the 3D printing industry.</p></p>
