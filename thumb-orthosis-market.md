<p><p>Thumb orthoses are devices used to support and protect the thumb during rehabilitation following injury or surgery. There are three main types of thumb orthoses in the market: metal stay splints, thermoplastic splints, and aluminum core splints. Metal stay splints provide rigid support using metal inserts, thermoplastic splints offer customizable support by molding to the thumb's shape, and aluminum core splints combine the durability of metal with the flexibility of thermoplastic. Each type caters to different needs and preferences of patients requiring thumb orthoses.</p></p>
