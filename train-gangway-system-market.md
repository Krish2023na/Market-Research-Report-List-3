<p><p>Train gangway systems can be categorized into two main types: single piece rail gangways and two piece rail gangways. Single piece rail gangways are designed as a continuous structure, providing a seamless connection between train cars. Both types offer unique advantages in terms of flexibility, efficiency, and safety in train operations.</p></p>
