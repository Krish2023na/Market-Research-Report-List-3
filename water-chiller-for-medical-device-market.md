<p><p>Water chiller for medical devices market can be broadly classified into two types- air-cooled water chillers and water-cooled water chillers. Air-cooled water chillers use air to dissipate heat from the refrigerant while water-cooled water chillers use water to remove heat. Air-cooled chillers are generally more cost-effective and easier to install, while water-cooled chillers are more energy-efficient and suitable for larger applications. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining stable temperatures for medical devices, ensuring their optimal performance and safety.</p></p>
