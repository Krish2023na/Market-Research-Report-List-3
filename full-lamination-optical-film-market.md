<p><ul><li>OCA</li><li>OCR</li><li>SCA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Full-lamination Optical Film market is segmented into three main types - OCA (Optically Clear Adhesive), OCR (Optically Clear Resin), and SCA (Structural Conductive Adhesive). OCA is commonly used for bonding display modules and touch panels, OCR provides better impact resistance and heat resistance, while SCA is specifically designed for touch panel applications requiring high capacitance and conductivity. Each type caters to specific needs in the display industry, offering various advantages and applications for manufacturers and consumers.</p></p>
