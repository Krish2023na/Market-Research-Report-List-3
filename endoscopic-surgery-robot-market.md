<p><p>Endoscopic Surgery Robot market can be categorized into two main types: Portrait Assistant Operating Endoscope and Endoscope Robot Operating Endoscope. </p><p>Portrait Assistant Operating Endoscope refers to a system where the robot assists the surgeon in holding and manipulating the endoscope during the procedure. This type provides increased stability and precision during surgery.</p><p>On the other hand, Endoscope Robot Operating Endoscope involves a robot that is designed to autonomously navigate and manipulate the endoscope inside the patient's body. This type allows for greater dexterity and control in hard-to-reach areas, enhancing the overall surgical experience.</p></p>
