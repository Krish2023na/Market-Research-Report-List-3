<p><p>Glass mold market is categorized into three main types: Ordinary Cast Iron Mold, Alloy Cast Iron Mold, and Other Material Mold. Alloy cast iron molds are more durable and have a longer lifespan compared to ordinary cast iron molds. Other material molds, such as ceramic or stainless steel, are used for specific applications where special properties are required. Each type of mold offers unique benefits and is chosen based on the specific needs of the glass manufacturer.</p></p>
