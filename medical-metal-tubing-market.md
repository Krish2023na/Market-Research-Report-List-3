<p><p>Medical metal tubing market consists of various types of materials such as stainless steel, titanium, and nitinol (NiTi). Stainless steel tubing is known for its durability and resistance to corrosion, making it suitable for a wide range of medical applications. Titanium tubing is lightweight, strong, and biocompatible, making it ideal for implants and surgical instruments. Nitinol tubing, a nickel-titanium alloy, has shape memory and superelastic properties, making it suitable for minimally invasive medical devices such as stents and guidewires.</p></p>
