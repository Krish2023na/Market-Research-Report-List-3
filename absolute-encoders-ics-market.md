<p><p>Absolute Encoders ICs are divided into two main types: Single-turn Encoders and Multi-turn Encoders. Single-turn Encoders provide an absolute output value for each position within one revolution, while Multi-turn Encoders provide absolute position values across multiple revolutions. Single-turn Encoders are suitable for applications that require precision within one revolution, while Multi-turn Encoders are ideal for applications that involve continuous rotation and require accurate position tracking over multiple revolutions. Both types offer high accuracy and reliability in various industrial and automation systems.</p></p>
