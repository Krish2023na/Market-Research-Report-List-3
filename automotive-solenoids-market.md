<p><p>Automotive solenoids come in various types, including 2-way valve, 3-way valve, 4-way valve, and 5-way valve. A 2-way valve controls the flow of fluid in one direction, while a 3-way valve can switch between two different flow paths. A 4-way valve can control the flow in two different directions, and a 5-way valve adds the ability to regulate the pressure in the system. Each type of solenoid serves a specific purpose in controlling the flow and direction of fluids in automotive systems.</p></p>
