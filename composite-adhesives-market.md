<p><p>Composite adhesives market is dominated by two main types - Epoxy and Polyurethane. Epoxy adhesives are known for their high strength, durability and resistance to chemicals, making them ideal for bonding materials like metals, ceramics and composites. On the other hand, Polyurethane adhesives offer flexibility, impact resistance and a strong bond, making them suitable for applications requiring vibration and thermal cycling resistance. Both types cater to a wide range of industries including automotive, aerospace, construction and electronics due to their superior bonding properties.</p></p>
