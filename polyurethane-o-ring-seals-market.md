<p><p>Non-Reinforced Seals in the polyurethane O-Ring market are made solely of polyurethane material without any added reinforcement. These types of seals are ideal for applications with low to moderate pressure requirements. </p><p>On the other hand, Fabric Reinforced Seals are enhanced with fabric or polyester reinforcement within the polyurethane material. These seals provide increased strength and durability, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. The fabric reinforcement helps prevent extrusion and improves overall seal performance in demanding environments.</p></p>
