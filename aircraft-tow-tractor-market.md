<p><p>Aircraft tow tractors are commonly categorized into two main types: conventional tractors and towbarless tractors. Conventional tractors use a towbar to connect with the aircraft for towing, while towbarless tractors utilize a lifting mechanism to securely grasp the aircraft's landing gear. Conventional tractors are more traditional and widely used, whereas towbarless tractors offer enhanced maneuverability and efficiency. Both types cater to different needs within the aviation industry, with towbarless tractors becoming increasingly popular due to their innovative design.</p></p>
