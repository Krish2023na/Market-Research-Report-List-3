<p><p>The Automobile Fuel Injector market is segmented into two main types: Gasoline Injection and Diesel Injection. Gasoline Injection involves a fuel injector that sprays fuel directly into the engine's combustion chamber to create a mix with air for ignition. On the other hand, Diesel Injection uses a fuel injector that operates at higher pressures to atomize diesel fuel for efficient combustion. Both types of fuel injectors play a crucial role in optimizing fuel efficiency and engine performance in automobiles.</p></p>
