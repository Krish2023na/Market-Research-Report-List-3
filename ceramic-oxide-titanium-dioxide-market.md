<p><p>Ceramic oxide titanium dioxide market is categorized into different types based on the crystal structure of the compound: Anatase Type and Rutile Type. Anatase type is known for its high photocatalytic activity and is widely used in environmental and industrial applications. Rutile type offers excellent UV resistance and is commonly used in paints, coatings, and plastics. Other types in the market include a combination of Anatase and Rutile, as well as specialty grades tailored for specific applications such as cosmetics and electronics.</p></p>
