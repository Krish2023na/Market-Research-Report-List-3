<p><p>Diamond slurries are used in various industries for polishing and grinding applications. They are categorized into different types based on the type of liquid used as a carrier. Water-based slurries are environmentally friendly and easy to clean. Lubricant-based slurries provide effective lubrication during the polishing process. Alcohol-based slurries offer fast drying times and are suitable for heat-sensitive materials. Oil-based slurries provide high lubrication properties for heavy-duty applications. Each type of diamond slurry caters to specific needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
