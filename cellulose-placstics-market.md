<p><p>Cellulose Plastics are categorized into different types based on the chemical composition and properties. These types include Butyrate, Acetate, Propionate, and Other market. Butyrate cellulose plastics are known for their high impact resistance and clarity. Acetate cellulose plastics are valued for their film-forming and moldable characteristics. Propionate cellulose plastics are popular for their excellent heat resistance and moisture-wicking properties. The "Other" market includes cellulose plastics with unique compositions and applications tailored to specific industry needs.</p></p>
