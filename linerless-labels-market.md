<p><p>Linerless labels are available in four main types based on the adhesive used: Water-Based, Solvent-Based, Hot Melt-Based, and UV Curable. Water-Based labels are environmentally friendly, while Solvent-Based labels offer good adhesion. Hot Melt-Based labels are suitable for high-speed applications and UV Curable labels provide excellent resistance to chemicals and solvents. Each type has its own advantages and applications, catering to different needs in the market. Overall, linerless labels offer a versatile and efficient solution for labeling requirements.</p></p>
