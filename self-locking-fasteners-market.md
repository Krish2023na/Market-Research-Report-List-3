<p><p>Self-locking fasteners market includes self-locking nuts, self-locking screws, and other types of fasteners designed to prevent loosening or backing out due to vibration or other factors. Self-locking nuts have a built-in locking mechanism such as nylon inserts or distorted threads to secure them in place. Self-locking screws have features like thread locking patches or serrated heads to prevent rotation. Other self-locking fasteners may include washers, bolts, or pins with similar locking mechanisms to ensure a secure connection in various applications.</p></p>
