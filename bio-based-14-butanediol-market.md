<p><p>Bio-based 1,4-Butanediol can be produced through two main market types: direct fermentation and biosuccinic acid. In direct fermentation, renewable feedstocks such as sugars or starches are used to produce 1,4-Butanediol through microbial fermentation processes. On the other hand, the biosuccinic acid market involves the production of bio-based 1,4-Butanediol through the fermentation of plant-based materials to produce succinic acid, which is then hydrogenated to form 1,4-Butanediol. Both market types offer sustainable alternatives to traditional petrochemical-based production methods.</p></p>
