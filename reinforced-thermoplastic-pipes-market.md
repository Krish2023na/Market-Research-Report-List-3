<p><p>Reinforced Thermoplastic Pipes (RTP) are used in various industries and come in two main types: Non-metallic RTP and Metallic RTP. Non-metallic RTP pipes are made from high-strength composite materials and are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and highly flexible, making them ideal for offshore and onshore applications. On the other hand, Metallic RTP pipes contain a layer of metal for added strength and are typically used in high-pressure applications. Both types of RTP pipes offer durability, long lifespan, and cost-effectiveness compared to traditional metal pipes.</p></p>
