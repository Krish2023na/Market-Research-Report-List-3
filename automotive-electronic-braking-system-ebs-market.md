<p><p>The Automotive Electronic Braking System (EBS) market includes two main types of braking systems: disc brake and drum brake. Disc brakes use calipers to squeeze brake pads against a rotor to slow down or stop the vehicle. On the other hand, drum brakes use brake shoes that press against the inner surface of a drum to achieve the same goal. Both types of braking systems are essential components of a vehicle's safety system, helping to ensure smooth and efficient braking performance.</p></p>
