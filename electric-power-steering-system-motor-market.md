<p><strong>The Electric Power Steering System Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CEPS Motor</li><li>REPS Motor</li><li>PEPS Motor</li><li>EHPS Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric Power Steering System Motors can be classified into various types based on their design and functionality. These include Column Electric Power Steering (CEPS) Motors, Rack Electric Power Steering (REPS) Motors, Pinion Electric Power Steering (PEPS) Motors, and Electric Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS) Motors. CEPS Motors are installed directly on the steering column, REPS Motors are attached to the steering rack, PEPS Motors are connected to the steering pinion, and EHPS Motors combine electric and hydraulic systems for efficient power steering assistance.</p></p>
