<p><strong>The Ammonia-Powered Internal Combustion Engine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spark-Ignition Engine</li><li>Compression-Ignition Engine</li><li>Gas-Turbine Engine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ammonia-powered internal combustion engines can be classified into three main types: </p><p>1. Spark-Ignition Engine: This type uses a spark plug to ignite a mixture of ammonia and air in the combustion chamber, similar to gasoline engines.</p><p>2. Compression-Ignition Engine: This type compresses a mixture of ammonia and air until it reaches a high enough temperature for spontaneous ignition, similar to diesel engines.</p><p>3. Gas-Turbine Engine: This type uses high-pressure gases produced by burning ammonia in a turbine to generate power, similar to jet engines.</p></p>
