<p><p>Transient-Voltage-Suppression (TVS) diodes are used to protect electronic circuits from voltage spikes. There are two main types: Uni-polar TVS diodes, which are designed to handle positive or negative voltage spikes, and Bi-polar TVS diodes, which can handle both positive and negative voltage spikes. Uni-polar TVS diodes are commonly used in applications where only one polarity of voltage spike needs to be suppressed, while Bi-polar TVS diodes are used when both positive and negative spikes need to be suppressed to protect the circuit.</p></p>
