<p><p>Round knitting looms are tools used for knitting in a circular or tubular fashion. They consist of a ring with pegs around the edge, allowing users to knit without the need for traditional knitting needles. These looms are popular among both beginners and experienced knitters, as they are easy to use and can create a variety of different projects such as hats, scarves, and even blankets.</p><p>The Round Knitting Looms Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.88% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing popularity of knitting as a hobby, as well as the rising demand for DIY crafts and handmade products. Additionally, the ease of use and versatility of round knitting looms make them an attractive option for knitters of all skill levels.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the round knitting looms market is the introduction of advanced looms with features such as adjustable peg spacing, interchangeable pegs, and ergonomic designs for increased comfort.
