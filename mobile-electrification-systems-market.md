<p><p>Mobile Electrification Systems are essential components in rail transit systems, providing power to moving vehicles. The Cable Hanging System involves overhead cables running parallel to the tracks, supplying power through a pantograph mechanism. The Cable Drum System uses a reel of cable that moves along with the vehicle, delivering power through a sliding contact. These systems are crucial for efficient operation of rail transit systems, ensuring continuous power supply to the moving vehicles for a smooth and reliable transportation service.</p></p>
