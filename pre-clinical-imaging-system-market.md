<p><strong>The Pre Clinical Imaging System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standalone Imaging System</li><li>Multimodal Imaging System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pre Clinical Imaging System market consists of two main types: Standalone Imaging System and Multimodal Imaging System. Standalone Imaging Systems are dedicated to a single imaging modality, such as MRI, CT, or PET, providing high-quality imaging for specific applications. Multimodal Imaging Systems, on the other hand, combine two or more imaging modalities in one system, allowing researchers to perform complementary imaging techniques for more comprehensive data analysis. Both types of systems play a crucial role in preclinical research for evaluating disease models and drug efficacy.</p></p>
