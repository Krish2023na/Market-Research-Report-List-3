<p><p>Passenger Vehicle Air Conditioner Market offers two main types: Reciprocating Type and Rotary Type. Reciprocating Type air conditioners use a piston compressor to compress the refrigerant and cool the air. They are typically more affordable but may consume more energy and make more noise. In contrast, Rotary Type air conditioners use a rotary compressor to achieve cooling. Although they are generally quieter and more efficient, they can be more expensive compared to Reciprocating Type systems. Both types cater to the needs of the passenger vehicle market.</p></p>
