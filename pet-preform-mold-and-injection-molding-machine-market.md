<p><p>PET preform mold is a tool used for producing preforms that are later on blown into bottles and containers for various liquid products. Injection molding machines are used to create the preforms by injecting molten PET resin into the mold cavity. These machines are used in the manufacturing process of PET bottles, containers, and other packaging items.</p><p>The PET preform mold and injection molding machine market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.4% during the forecast period. The growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for PET bottles and containers in the beverage, food, and pharmaceutical industries. The rise in disposable income, changing consumer lifestyles, and growing awareness about the benefits of PET packaging are also driving the market growth.</p><p>Moreover, technological advancements in injection molding machines, such as automation and digitalization, are further fueling the market growth.
