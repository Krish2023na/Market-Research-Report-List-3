<p><strong>The Medical Sewage Treatment Plants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Preliminary Stage or Pretreatment</li><li>Primary Treatment Stage</li><li>Secondary Treatment Stage</li><li>Tertiary or Advanced Treatment Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical sewage treatment plants consist of various stages to effectively treat wastewater. The preliminary stage or pretreatment involves screening and grit removal to separate large solids. The primary treatment stage involves physical processes like sedimentation to remove suspended solids. The secondary treatment stage uses biological processes to further break down organic matter. The tertiary or advanced treatment stage includes advanced filtration methods like activated carbon or disinfection processes to remove pathogens and contaminants. Each stage plays a crucial role in ensuring the wastewater is safely treated before discharge.</p></p>
