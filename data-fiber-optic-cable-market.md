<p><p>Data Fiber Optic Cable market includes two main types - Single Mode and Multimode. Single Mode cables are designed for longer distances and higher bandwidth, making them ideal for long-distance communication applications such as telecommunications and data centers. On the other hand, Multimode cables are best suited for shorter distance connections within buildings or campuses, offering lower bandwidth but greater flexibility and cost-effectiveness. Both types play a crucial role in supporting the increasing demand for high-speed data transmission in various industries.</p></p>
