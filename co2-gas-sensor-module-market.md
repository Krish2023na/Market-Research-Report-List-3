<p><strong>The CO2 Gas Sensor Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) CO2 Sensors</li><li>EC (Electrochemical) CO2 Sensors</li><li>PAS (Photoacoustic Spectroscopy) CO2 Sensors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CO2 Gas Sensor Modules are categorized into different types based on the technology used for detecting carbon dioxide levels in the environment. NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) CO2 Sensors operate by measuring the absorption of infrared light by CO2 molecules. EC (Electrochemical) CO2 Sensors utilize a chemical reaction to generate an electrical signal. PAS (Photoacoustic Spectroscopy) CO2 Sensors detect CO2 by measuring the sound waves produced when it absorbs light. Other types of CO2 sensors may use different mechanisms for detecting carbon dioxide levels.</p></p>
