<p><p>Waterproof flooring market includes various types such as Three Layer and Multilayer flooring. Three Layer flooring consists of three layers - a top wear layer, a middle decorative layer, and a bottom waterproof layer. Multilayer flooring, on the other hand, is made up of multiple layers that are typically more durable and stable than Three Layer flooring. Both types of waterproof flooring offer protection against water damage and are popular choices for areas prone to moisture such as bathrooms and kitchens.</p></p>
