<p><p>Titanium Diboride market has two main types of production methods, including Carbothermal reduction and Self-propagating Reaction (SHS). Carbothermal reduction involves the reaction of titanium dioxide and boron oxide with carbon at high temperatures to produce titanium diboride. On the other hand, SHS production method involves the exothermic reaction between titanium and boron powders without the need for external heating. Both methods have their advantages and applications in different industries such as aerospace, electronics, and automotive.</p></p>
