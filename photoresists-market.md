<p><p>Photoresists are light-sensitive materials used in the semiconductor industry for photolithography processes. ARF Immersion Photoresist is designed for advanced technology nodes using ArF immersion lithography. ARF Dry Photoresist is used for ArF dry lithography processes. KRF Photoresist is suitable for KrF lithography systems. G-Line & I-Line Photoresist are used in older generation lithography systems such as G-Line and I-Line.
