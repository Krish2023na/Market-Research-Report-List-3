<p><p>Nematicides are categorized into two main types: liquid nematicides and dry nematicides. Liquid nematicides are typically applied as solutions or suspensions using equipment such as sprayers. They are easier to apply and distribute evenly in the soil. On the other hand, dry nematicides are solid formulations that need to be incorporated into the soil or applied as granules. They are known for their longer shelf life and convenient storage. Both types of nematicides are used by farmers to control and manage nematode infestations in crops.</p></p>
