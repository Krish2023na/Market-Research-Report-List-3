<p><p>Trim systems market includes trim tabs and interceptors. Trim tabs are adjustable devices on the trailing edge of a boat's hull that help control the boat's balance and stability by adjusting the angle of the tabs. Interceptors are similar to trim tabs but are mounted on the boat's transom and provide more precise control over the boat's trim and performance. Both trim tabs and interceptors are essential for improving a boat's handling in various conditions and optimizing fuel efficiency.</p></p>
