<p><p>Grease fittings, also known as zerk fittings, come in three main types: straight, 45-degree, and 90-degree. Straight grease fittings are ideal for easy access to lubrication points, while 45-degree fittings are often used in tight spaces where a straight fitting may not fit. 90-degree fittings are best suited for applications in which a flexible hose or elbow adapter is needed to reach the lubrication point. Each type of grease fitting offers unique advantages depending on the specific application and accessibility requirements.</p></p>
