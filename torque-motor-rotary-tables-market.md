<p><p>Torque motor rotary tables are divided into two main types based on their structure: external rotation structure and internal rotation structure. External rotation structures feature a stationary base with a rotating top plate, making them suitable for applications where the base needs to be fixed. Internal rotation structures have a rotating base and stationary top plate, making them ideal for situations where the top plate must remain stable. Both types offer unique benefits and cater to different needs in various industries.</p></p>
