<p><ul><li>Isolated Type PWM Switching Regulators</li><li>Non Isolated Type PWM Switching Regulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PWM (Pulse Width Modulation) switching regulators are divided into two main types: isolated and non-isolated. Isolated type regulators provide electrical isolation between the input and output, making them suitable for applications where safety and reliability are crucial, such as in medical or industrial equipment. Non-isolated regulators, on the other hand, do not provide electrical isolation but are more cost-effective and simpler to design. Both types are widely used in various industries for efficient power conversion and voltage regulation.</p></p>
