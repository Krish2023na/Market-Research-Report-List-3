<p><p>Mobile Virtual Network Operator (MVNO) market is divided into three types - Reseller, Service Operator, and Full MVNO. Resellers buy network services in bulk and sell them to end-users under their own brand. Service Operators provide additional services on top of network services, such as billing or customer support. Full MVNOs have full control over their network infrastructure and services, allowing them to offer more customized solutions to their customers. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
