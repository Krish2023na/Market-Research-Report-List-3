<p><p>The automotive air intake module market consists of three main types: cold air intake, ram air intake, and short ram intake. </p><p>Cold air intake modules are designed to bring cooler air into the engine for increased efficiency and power. </p><p>Ram air intake modules utilize the vehicle's forward motion to force more air into the engine, increasing performance. </p><p>Short ram intake modules are more compact and bring in air from the engine bay, providing a balance between power and space efficiency. Each type caters to different preferences and requirements in the automotive air intake market.</p></p>
