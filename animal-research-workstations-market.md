<p><p>Single channel and dual channel animal research workstations differ based on the number of channels available for conducting experiments. In single channel workstations, only one channel is available for performing experiments on animals, while dual channel workstations have two separate channels for conducting parallel experiments on different animals. Single channel workstations are suitable for simpler experiments where only one test is required, while dual channel workstations are more ideal for complex experiments that involve multiple tests or comparisons.</p></p>
