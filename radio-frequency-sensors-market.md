<p><strong>The Radio Frequency Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive</li><li>Active</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radio Frequency Sensors can be categorized into two main types: Passive and Active. Passive RF sensors do not require an external power source and operate by detecting and responding to external RF signals, such as radar. On the other hand, Active RF sensors use their own power source to emit electromagnetic waves and detect the reflected signals. Both types of RF sensors are used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and healthcare for applications like object detection, temperature sensing, and security monitoring.</p></p>
