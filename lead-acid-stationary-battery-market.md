<p><ul><li>General Storage Battery</li><li>Dry Charged Battery</li><li>Maintenance Free Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lead Acid Stationary Battery market is divided into three types: General Storage Battery, Dry Charged Battery, and Maintenance Free Battery. General Storage Batteries are traditional lead acid batteries that require regular maintenance and topping up with distilled water. Dry Charged Batteries come without electrolyte and require filling before use, making them more convenient for storage and transportation. Maintenance Free Batteries are sealed and require no topping up of electrolyte, offering a hassle-free solution for stationary power applications.</p></p>
