<p><strong>The Automobile Refinish Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Borne</li><li>Water Borne</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile refinish paint market is categorized into three types: solvent borne, water borne, and others. Solvent borne paints use organic solvents to disperse the pigments, providing a glossy and durable finish. Water borne paints use water as a solvent, making them more environmentally friendly and easy to clean. The "others" category includes high solid coatings, powder coatings, and UV-cured coatings, offering unique properties and applications in the automobile refinish paint market. Each type has its own advantages and uses in the industry.</p></p>
