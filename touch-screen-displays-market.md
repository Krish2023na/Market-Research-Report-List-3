<p><ul><li>Resistive Type</li><li>Capacitive Type</li><li>Infrared Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Touch screen displays are categorized into different types based on their technology. Resistive type displays use pressure to detect touch, making them durable and cost-effective. Capacitive type displays use electrical signals to detect touch, providing high clarity and responsiveness. Infrared type displays use infrared light to detect touch, offering accurate and fast touch responses. Other types of touch screen displays include surface acoustic wave (SAW) and optical sensors, each offering unique features and functionality for specific applications.</p></p>
