<p><p>Salon use hair colors and dyes are categorized into three main types: temporary, semi-permanent, and permanent. Temporary hair dye provides a quick color change that washes out after a few shampoos. Semi-permanent hair dye lasts longer, typically up to 12 washes. Permanent hair dye penetrates the hair shaft and remains until new hair growth. Each type offers a different level of commitment and color intensity, catering to a wide range of preferences and needs in the cosmetic industry.</p></p>
