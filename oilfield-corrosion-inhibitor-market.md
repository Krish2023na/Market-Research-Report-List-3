<p><p>Oilfield corrosion inhibitors are essential in preventing degradation of equipment and pipelines in the oil and gas industry. There are two main types of corrosion inhibitors in the market. </p><p>Organic corrosion inhibitors are composed of carbon-based molecules that form a protective film on the metal surface, preventing corrosive agents from reaching the metal. </p><p>Inorganic corrosion inhibitors are based on inorganic compounds such as zinc, phosphate, or chromate, which provide a physical barrier against corrosion. Both types of inhibitors are crucial in maintaining the integrity of oilfield infrastructure.</p></p>
