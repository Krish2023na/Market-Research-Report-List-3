<p><strong>The Inter Cooler / Charge Air Cooler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-Cooled Charge Air Cooler</li><li>Liquid-Cooled Charge Air Cooler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air-cooled charge air coolers use ambient air to cool the compressed air from the turbocharger before it enters the engine. These coolers are more compact and cost-effective, making them suitable for smaller vehicles. On the other hand, liquid-cooled charge air coolers use a coolant such as water or a mix of water and glycol to dissipate heat from the compressed air. These coolers are more efficient at cooling the air and are commonly used in high-performance vehicles or heavy-duty applications.</p></p>
