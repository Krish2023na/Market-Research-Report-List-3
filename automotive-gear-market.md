<p><p>The automotive gear market includes various types such as spur gears, bevel gears, screw gears, miter gears, and others. Spur gears are widely used in applications requiring high power transmission with minimal noise. Bevel gears are commonly used in differentials and power transmission systems with intersecting shafts. Screw gears are used for high torque applications. Miter gears are used for perpendicular shaft power transmission. Other types of gears in the market include helical gears, worm gears, and planetary gears, each serving specific functions in automotive applications.</p></p>
