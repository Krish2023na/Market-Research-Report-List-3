<p><p>The automotive 2-D HUD market includes two main types: combiner and windshield HUD. Combiner HUDs project information onto a transparent screen that flips down in front of the driver's line of sight. Windshield HUDs display information directly onto the windshield itself, allowing the driver to see data without taking their eyes off the road. Each type offers its own advantages and appeal to different consumers based on preferences and needs for vehicle information display.</p></p>
