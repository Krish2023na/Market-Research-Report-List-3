<p><p>Ceramic matrix composites can be classified into various types based on the materials used in the matrix and reinforcement. Oxide/Oxide composites utilize oxide ceramics in both the matrix and reinforcement. SiC/SiC composites consist of silicon carbide in both components. Carbon/Carbon composites feature carbon-based materials in the matrix and reinforcement. Other types of ceramic matrix composites may use various combinations of materials. Each type offers unique properties and benefits, catering to different applications in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and energy.</p></p>
