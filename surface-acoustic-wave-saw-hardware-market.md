<p><p>Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) hardware market includes various types of products such as filters, correctors, converters, delay lines, oscillators, and others. Filters are used to eliminate unwanted signals, correctors are used to adjust the phase or frequency of signals, converters are used to change signal formats, delay lines introduce time delays in signals, and oscillators generate stable frequencies. Other SAW hardware products include transducers, resonators, and sensors. Each type of hardware plays a crucial role in various electronic applications.</p></p>
