<p><p>Laminated steel containers come in two main types: 2-piece cans and 3-piece cans. 2-piece cans are made from a single piece of steel that is formed into a cylinder with a bottom end attached, while 3-piece cans are made from three separate pieces - a body, top, and bottom. 2-piece cans are mainly used for beverages and food products, while 3-piece cans are preferred for more specialized applications such as aerosol sprays and paint. Both types offer strong protection against external elements and are widely used in packaging industries.</p></p>
