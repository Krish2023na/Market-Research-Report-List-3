<p><p>Thyroid cancer molecular diagnostics market includes various types such as PCR, FISH, DNA sequencing, and gene chip technologies. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) is used to amplify specific DNA sequences, while FISH (Fluorescence In Situ Hybridization) is used to detect chromosomal abnormalities. DNA sequencing helps in identifying mutations in specific genes related to thyroid cancer. Gene chip technology allows for the simultaneous analysis of multiple genes. Each type plays a crucial role in the accurate diagnosis and treatment selection for thyroid cancer patients.</p></p>
