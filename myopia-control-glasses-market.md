<p><p>Myopia Control Glasses market offers two main types of lenses, Polycarbonate Lens and Resin Lens. Polycarbonate lenses are durable, lightweight, and impact-resistant, making them ideal for kids and athletes. These lenses also provide UV protection. On the other hand, Resin lenses are known for their high refractive index, resulting in thinner and lighter lenses compared to traditional lenses. Both types of lenses are highly effective in controlling myopia progression and offering clear vision to individuals with nearsightedness.</p></p>
