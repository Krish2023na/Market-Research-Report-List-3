<p><p>Unmanned Aircraft Systems (UAS) can be classified into two main types: Fixed Wing and Rotary Wing. Fixed Wing UAS have a fixed wing structure similar to traditional airplanes, providing longer endurance and higher speed capabilities. Rotary Wing UAS, on the other hand, have rotating blades like helicopters, allowing for vertical takeoff and landing capabilities and better maneuverability in confined spaces. Both types of UAS have unique advantages and applications in various industries including surveillance, agriculture, and infrastructure inspection.</p></p>
