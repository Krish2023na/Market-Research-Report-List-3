<p><p>Rapid Strength Concrete is categorized into three main market types based on their compressive strength: Under C30, C30-C60, and Above C60. Under C30 refers to concrete with a compressive strength of less than 30 megapascals, suitable for general-purpose projects. C30-C60 includes concrete with a range of compressive strengths, ideal for structural applications. Above C60 consists of high-strength concrete with compressive strength exceeding 60 megapascals, used for heavy-duty construction projects that require superior durability and performance. Each market type caters to specific project requirements and performance needs.</p></p>
