<p><ul><li>Infrared Photoelectric Sensor</li><li>Microwave Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic sliding door sensor market consists of two main types: infrared photoelectric sensors and microwave sensors. Infrared photoelectric sensors use beams of light to detect when someone is approaching the door, triggering it to open. Microwave sensors, on the other hand, use radio waves to detect movement and open the door accordingly. Both types of sensors are commonly used in commercial and residential buildings to provide hands-free entry and improve accessibility and convenience for users.</p></p>
