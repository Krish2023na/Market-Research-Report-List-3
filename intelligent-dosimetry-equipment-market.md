<p><ul><li>Thermoluminescent Dosimetry (TLD)</li><li>Optically Stimulated Luminescence (OSL)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Intelligent Dosimetry Equipment market includes Thermoluminescent Dosimetry (TLD) and Optically Stimulated Luminescence (OSL) types. TLD measures ionizing radiation exposure by detecting light emitted from crystalline materials when heated. OSL, on the other hand, detects radiation exposure by measuring light emitted when a material is stimulated with light. Both technologies are crucial in monitoring radiation exposure for workers in nuclear power plants, medical facilities, and other industries where exposure to ionizing radiation is a concern.</p></p>
