This type of ablation therapy is minimally invasive and is often used to treat conditions such as liver, lung, and kidney tumors. On the other hand, Cold Cycle Ablation market includes devices that use cycles of heating and cooling to ablate tissue. This method is less damaging to surrounding healthy tissue and is commonly used in the treatment of cardiac arrhythmias.</p></p>
