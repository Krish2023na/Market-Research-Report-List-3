<p><p>Integrated Starter Generator (ISG) is a technology used in vehicles to combine the functions of a starter motor and a generator into one unit. In a Series Hybrid market, the ISG is the primary source of power for the vehicle, with an internal combustion engine used only to charge the battery. In a Parallel Hybrid market, the ISG works alongside the internal combustion engine to power the vehicle. In a Series-Parallel Hybrid market, the ISG can operate in both series and parallel modes, allowing for more flexibility in power distribution.</p></p>
