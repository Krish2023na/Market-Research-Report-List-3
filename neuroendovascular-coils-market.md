<p><p>Neuroendovascular coils are commonly used in the treatment of cerebral aneurysms. There are two main types of coils available in the market - bare platinum coils and coated bioactive coils. Bare platinum coils are made of platinum and are usually used for simple aneurysm cases. On the other hand, coated bioactive coils are infused with a bioactive material that promotes faster and more effective aneurysm healing. Both types of coils have their own set of advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the patient's condition.</p></p>
