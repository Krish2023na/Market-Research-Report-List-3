<p><p>The Tower Crane market consists of various types of cranes including Self-erecting Tower Cranes, Flat Top Tower Cranes, Hammerhead Tower Cranes, and Luffing Jib Tower Cranes. Self-erecting Tower Cranes are easy to transport and assemble quickly on site. Flat Top Tower Cranes have a compact design and are suitable for congested job sites. Hammerhead Tower Cranes have a fixed horizontal jib and are known for their high lifting capacities. Luffing Jib Tower Cranes have a flexible jib that can move up and down, making them ideal for urban construction projects.</p></p>
