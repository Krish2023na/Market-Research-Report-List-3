<p><p>Tire sealants are divided into two main types: latex-based sealants and latex-free sealants. Latex-based sealants use natural rubber latex as their primary ingredient, providing effective sealing properties. On the other hand, latex-free sealants are made without latex, making them suitable for individuals with latex allergies. Both types of sealants are designed to quickly and easily seal punctures in tires, preventing air loss and flat tires. Consumers can choose between these two options based on their preferences and needs.</p></p>
