<p><p>Fire-Rated Building Material market includes various types such as Sealants & Fillers, Mortar, Sheets/Boards, Spray, Preformed Device, Putty, Cast-in Devices, and others. Sealants & Fillers are used to seal gaps and joints to prevent fire spread, while Mortar is used to fill joints and gaps in masonry constructions. Sheets/Boards provide fire protection for walls and ceilings, while Spray is applied to structural elements like steel beams. Preformed Devices are prefabricated fireproof elements, Putty provides sealant and insulation, and Cast-in Devices are integrated into concrete structures for fire protection.</p></p>
