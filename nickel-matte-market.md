<p><p>Nickel matte can be produced through various market types including layered smelting, leaching, carbonyl process, and flotation process. Layered smelting involves separating nickel and other metals through a series of smelting processes. Leaching involves dissolving nickel using acids or other solvents. The carbonyl process involves converting nickel into nickel carbonyl gas and then decomposing it to retrieve pure nickel. The flotation process uses chemical reagents to separate nickel from other materials through a process of bubbling air through a mixture.</p></p>
