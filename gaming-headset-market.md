<p><p>The gaming headset market can be categorized into various types based on their design and functionality. Supra-Aural headsets are compact and rest on the ears, while Circumaural headsets have larger cups that fully enclose the ears. Canalphones fit inside the ear canals for a more secure fit, while Backphones have speakers that rest against the back of the outer ear. Other types of gaming headsets include bone conduction and wireless options, offering different features and styles to cater to diverse user preferences.</p></p>
