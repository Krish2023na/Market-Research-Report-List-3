<p><p>Heat shield market is divided into two main types based on their material composition and application methods - rigid heat shields and flexible heat shields. Rigid heat shields are typically made of materials like aluminum or stainless steel, providing excellent thermal protection in high temperature environments. On the other hand, flexible heat shields are usually made of materials like fiberglass or ceramic fibers, offering versatility and easy installation in tight spaces. Both types play a crucial role in protecting components from heat damage in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics.</p></p>
