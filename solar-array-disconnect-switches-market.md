<p><p>Solar array disconnect switches are crucial components in solar energy systems for safety and maintenance purposes. The AC disconnect switch is designed to disconnect the alternating current (AC) output of the solar array from the grid or inverter for maintenance or emergency shutdown. On the other hand, the DC disconnect switch is responsible for disconnecting the direct current (DC) output of the solar panels from the inverter or charge controller. Both types of disconnect switches are essential for efficient and safe operation of solar energy systems.</p></p>
