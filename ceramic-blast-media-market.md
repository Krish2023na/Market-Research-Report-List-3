<p><p>Ceramic Blast Media Market Types refer to different forms of ceramic materials used in the blasting industry. Ceramic Sand is a type of blast media made from refined bauxite and fired at a high temperature, creating a sharp and durable abrasive material. Meanwhile, Ceramic Shot is made from spherical beads of ceramic material, providing a more uniform finish on the surface being blasted. Both types of ceramic media are popular choices for industries requiring precision surface preparation and finishing.</p></p>
