<p><p>Plant nutritional lipids market comprises different types such as phospholipids and sterols. Phospholipids are essential components of cell membranes and play a crucial role in various biological processes. They are widely used in food and pharmaceutical industries for their emulsifying and stabilizing properties. On the other hand, sterols are important for maintaining cell membrane structure and are believed to have cholesterol-lowering effects. Both phospholipids and sterols are gaining traction in the market due to their health benefits and increasing consumer awareness about plant-based nutrition.</p></p>
