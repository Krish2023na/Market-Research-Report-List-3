<p><strong>The Fluorine-Containing Polymer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Perfluoropolymer Type</li><li>Partially Fluorinated Polymer Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluorine-containing polymers can be classified into two main types: perfluoropolymer and partially fluorinated polymer. Perfluoropolymers, such as PTFE and PFA, have all hydrogen atoms replaced with fluorine atoms, making them highly resistant to chemicals, heat, and weathering. Partially fluorinated polymers, like PVDF and ETFE, have some hydrogen atoms replaced with fluorine, offering a balance of properties like flexibility, strength, and chemical resistance. Both types of fluorine-containing polymers find uses in a wide range of industries including automotive, electronics, and semiconductor.</p></p>
