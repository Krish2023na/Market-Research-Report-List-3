<p><p>Electrical substations are classified into different types based on their design and insulation method. AIS (Air Insulated Substation) uses air as the primary dielectric insulating medium, while GIS (Gas Insulated Substation) uses gas such as sulfur hexafluoride for insulation. The "Others" market includes substations using different insulation methods or technologies. These different types of substations cater to varying needs and requirements in the power distribution and transmission sector, offering solutions for different environmental and operational conditions.</p></p>
