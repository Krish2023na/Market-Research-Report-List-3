<p><p>Fuel cell market is categorized into different types based on the technology used. Proton Exchange Membrane Fuel Cells (PEMFCs) are commonly used in transportation applications. Solid Oxide Fuel Cells (SOFC) are widely used in stationary power generation. Molten Carbonate Fuel Cells (MCFC) are used in large-scale power plants. Phosphoric Acid Fuel Cells (PAFC) are suitable for cogeneration of heat and power. Other types of fuel cells include Direct Methanol Fuel Cells (DMFCs) and Alkaline Fuel Cells (AFCs) used in niche applications. Each type offers specific advantages for different uses.</p></p>
