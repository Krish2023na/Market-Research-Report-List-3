<p><p>Nonfat dry milk powder can be categorized into three different market types based on the heat used during the drying process. Low heat nonfat dry milk powder is produced using gentle heat to preserve more of the milk's natural flavor and nutrients. Medium heat nonfat dry milk powder undergoes a slightly higher temperature during drying, resulting in a more pronounced milk flavor. High heat nonfat dry milk powder is processed at a higher temperature, which gives it a darker color and a stronger cooked milk flavor.</p></p>
