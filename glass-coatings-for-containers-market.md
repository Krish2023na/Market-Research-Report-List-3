<p><ul><li>Hot-end Coatings</li><li>Cold-end Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Glass coatings for containers are broadly categorized into hot-end coatings and cold-end coatings. Hot-end coatings are applied during the manufacturing process to protect the glass from chemical reactions and improve mechanical strength. Cold-end coatings, on the other hand, are applied after production to enhance the container's resistance to scratching, chemicals, and abrasion. Both types of coatings play a crucial role in extending the lifespan and functionality of glass containers in various industries.</p></p>
