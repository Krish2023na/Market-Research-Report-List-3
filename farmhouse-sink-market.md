<p><p>Farmhouse sinks are available in different types based on installation methods. Drop-in sinks are easy to install as they are dropped into a pre-cut hole in the countertop, creating a visible rim above the surface. Under-mount sinks are installed under the countertop, providing a seamless look with no rims or edges visible. Both types offer different aesthetic and functional advantages, catering to different preferences and kitchen designs. Customers can choose between drop-in or under-mount sinks depending on their needs and desired style for their farmhouse sink.</p></p>
