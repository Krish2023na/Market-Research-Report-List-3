<p><strong>The Solar Control Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Clear (Non-Reflective)</li><li>Dyed (Non-Reflective)</li><li>Vacuum Coated (Reflective)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solar control films are categorized into three main types: Clear (Non-Reflective), Dyed (Non-Reflective), and Vacuum Coated (Reflective). Clear films allow natural light transmission while reducing heat and glare. Dyed films have added color for aesthetic purposes and provide moderate heat reduction. Vacuum Coated films have a reflective surface that reflects heat and UV rays, providing the highest level of solar control. Each type offers varying levels of heat reduction, glare reduction, and UV protection, catering to different preferences and requirements.</p></p>
